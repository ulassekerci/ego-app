# EGO Branding

### Logo

Ego has a logo with 2 color variants (red and white) and 2 text variants (with and without EGO Genel Müdürlüğü text). You can find all 4 variants in xcode assets. Logo should be 40px minimum to maintain readability and white logo should be placed over either red or black

### Color

Ego brand colors are the turkish flag red (#E30A17) and white (#FFFFFF).
